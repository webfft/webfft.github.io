Vowels Visualizer

A few examples (images are clickable):

Steps:

  1. Record a vowel sound using your mic or upload a recording.
  2. Click zoom-in and zoom-out to adjust the sample rate.
  3. Increase or decrease brightness of the image.
Audio is processed on the client.

This is the autocorrelation spectrogram mapped to a circle. Compare (images are clickable):

  1. Image 1. The good old spectrogram that shows (re^2+im^2) values of the DFT. One column represents ~150 ms of audio.
  2. Image 2. One more DFT is applied to each column of the spectrogram. This is how the autocorrelation of signal[x*T..(x+1)*T] is computed.
  3. Image 3. Finally the image is mapped to a circle.
